How It Works

From call to deposit in minutes

1
A customer calls your number 24/7
Tideline answers. It asks the right questions — LOA, arrival dates, boat make and model — and collects everything your team would normally write down.
2
Your team gets a text summary Instant
Tideline sends a clean SMS to your staff: customer name, boat info, dates, request type. Reply YES to confirm, NO to decline.
3
Customer gets notified right away Auto
For transient slip approvals, Tideline texts the customer a Stripe deposit link — no callback needed. For service requests, they get a confirmation.
4
Collect final payment via Tideline PAY Integrated
When the work is complete, text PAY [customer] [amount] to send a payment link straight to their phone. Funds go directly to your Stripe account.
5
Everything is logged Always
Every call, request, and message is recorded with timestamps, boat details, and payment status.
